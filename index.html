FAQ

Popular Questions About Moving to Shanghai

What apps do I need before arriving in Shanghai?

Start with WeChat, Alipay, Didi, Baidu Maps, and a translation app. Install and test them before you fly.

Read the full guide →
Do I need a VPN in China?

If you rely on overseas services for work, school, travel, or personal accounts, yes. Install and test one before arrival.

Read the full guide →
Can foreigners use Alipay and WeChat Pay?

Yes. Foreign cards can link to both apps, though verification steps and card limits vary by bank.

Read the full guide →
How do I get from Shanghai airport to the city?

From Pudong airport, take the Maglev or Metro Line 2; from Hongqiao, Metro Line 2 or 10 connect straight to the city.

Read the full guide →
Which neighbourhood is best for newcomers?

Jing'an is a popular first base for its central location, metro access, and easy day-to-day errands.

Read the full guide →
Where can I buy foreign groceries in Shanghai?

International supermarkets, specialty importers, and grocery delivery apps all carry familiar imported food.
