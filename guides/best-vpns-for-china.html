Practical Tips

VPN tips before arriving in China

Do the boring setup before your flight. It is much easier than troubleshooting after landing.

  1. 01Install before arrival

    Download your VPN apps, save login details securely, and test on your phone and laptop.

  2. 02Consider a backup

    VPN performance can vary by city, hotel Wi-Fi, mobile provider, network, and time of day.

  3. 03Prepare offline tools

    Download offline maps, translation apps, travel documents, and keep your VPN app updated.

FAQ

China VPN questions newcomers ask first

Do I need a VPN before arriving in China?

If you rely on overseas services for work, school, banking, travel, or personal accounts, install and test a VPN before arrival.

Which VPN is best for Shanghai?

For most newcomers, Surfshark is a strong value starting point, NordVPN is a polished all-round option, and Astrill is the premium long-term China pick.

Is Astrill VPN worth it for China?

It can be worth considering for long-term residents who prioritize China-specific reliability and are comfortable paying more.

Is Surfshark good for China?

Surfshark is a strong value recommendation for newcomers, but you should install and test it before traveling.

Should I use more than one VPN in China?

A backup option can be useful because VPN performance can vary by network, city, device, and timing.

Can I download a VPN after arriving in China?

It may be harder after arrival, so download, log in, and test your VPN before entering mainland China.

Are free VPNs recommended for China?

Free VPNs are usually not a good first choice for China because reliability, speed, privacy, and support can be limited.

Does VPN performance change in China?

Yes. Performance can change with location, network, device, hotel Wi-Fi, mobile provider, and time of day.
